steps to make a paper airplane

get ready to start creating

STEP 1:YOU FIRST NEED HAVE A RECTANGULAR SHEET OF PAPER.
STEP 2:FOLD THE PAPER HOROSONTALLY AND GRAB THE TOP TWO CORNERS AND FOLD THEM INTO TRIANGLES
(THE TRIANGLES END AT THE CENTER.)
STEP 3: HOLD BOTH TRIANGLES AND FOLD THEM DOWN.IT STOPS AT THE CENTER
STEP 4:FOLD THE BOTTOM POINT A LITTLE UP.
STEP 5:TAKE ONE HALF OF BIG TRIANGLE AND FOLD IT BACKWARDS.DO THE SAME THING WITH THE OTHER HALF.
NOW YOU MADE THE WINGS. SO GET READY TO FLY YOUR AIRPLANE. REMEMBER TO FOLD EVERYTING CAREFULLY.
